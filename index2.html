ABOUT

S S DRONE DYNAMICS

A drone, also called an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is a broad term that
refers to an aircraft that operates autonomously or by remote control, with no
pilot on board. Drones are usually small-or-medium-sized, and these ‘flying
robots’ can carry out a wide range of tasks, from stealth military operations to
package delivery to aerial photography.
